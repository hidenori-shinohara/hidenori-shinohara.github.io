# Proposition
Show that $\mathbb{R}[x]/\ev{x^2 - 4x + 3}$ is not an integral domain.

# Solution
$x - 3 + \ev{x^2 - 4x + 3}, x - 1 + \ev{x^2 - 4x + 3}$ are both nonzero elements.
However, their product is $0$.
